Welcome to my portfolio! My name is Kris Salinas. I mainly draw 2d art in a cartoon style. I prefer to draw characters and am interested in character design. I'm a student at Illinois State University studying Game Design with a minor in Psychology. I majored in psychology for 2.5 years before switching to game design, so I have a good amount of knowledge on psychological topics and how to conduct research, and enjoy applying psychological topics to my own games and art.

This particular drawing is fan art of characters from a game called Everhood developed by Foreign Gnomes.
